================================================================================
                                 CLASS STATS
================================================================================
This document lists the base stats, basic attacks, and abilities for all classes
in Arena.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. WARRIOR
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Name:         Warrior
Role:         Melee Bruiser
Description:  A durable frontliner who wades in and erupts with shockwaves.
Base Stats:
  - Health:        420 HP
  - Mana:          80 MP
  - Move Speed:    9 units/sec
  - Attack Power:  45 (UI rating)
  - Difficulty:    1 / 3 (Easy)

Basic Attack (Auto-Attack):
  - Kind:          Melee
  - Range:         2.6 units
  - Damage:        11
  - Cooldown:      800 ms (1.25 attacks per second)

Abilities:
  - [Q] Cleave
    Aim:           Self
    Cooldown:      1000 ms
    Mana Cost:     10 MP
    Range:         4 units
    Damage:        25
    Radius:        4 units (AoE, 180 degree arc in front of the caster)
    Effects:       Instant frontal sweep dealing 25 damage.

  - [W] Charge
    Aim:           Direction
    Cooldown:      5000 ms
    Mana Cost:     20 MP
    Range:         12 units
    Damage:        10
    Effects:       A fast gap-closing dash (speed: 34) that deals 10 damage to enemies in the path.

  - [E] Shield Wall
    Aim:           Self
    Cooldown:      12000 ms
    Mana Cost:     40 MP
    Effects:       Applies a 60 HP shield for 5000 ms.

  - [R] Ground Slam
    Aim:           Self
    Cooldown:      14000 ms
    Mana Cost:     60 MP
    Range:         5 units
    Damage:        60
    Radius:        5 units (AoE)
    Effects:       Deals 60 damage and knocks back hit targets (distance: 4, speed: 24).

    Aim:           Point

    Mana Cost:     30 MP
    Range:         16 units
    Damage:        26
    Radius:        2 units (AoE)

    Aim:           Self
    Cooldown:      10000 ms
    Mana Cost:     60 MP
    Range:         8 units

    Range:         18 units
    Damage:        6 per tick
    Effects:       Channels a beam (18 range, 1 width) for 3000 ms, dealing 6 damage
                   every 200 ms to enemies inside. Caster can steer the beam.

5. NINJA
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Name:         Ninja
Role:         Melee Assassin
Description:  A swift, elusive shadow who strikes with a katana and smoke bombs.
Base Stats:
  - Health:        300 HP
  - Mana:          120 MP
  - Move Speed:    9 units/sec
  - Attack Power:  30 (UI rating)
  - Difficulty:    3 / 3 (Hard)

Basic Attack (Auto-Attack):
  - Kind:          Melee
  - Range:         2.6 units
  - Damage:        10
  - Cooldown:      700 ms (1.43 attacks per second)

Abilities:
  - [Q] Katana Slash (Double Slash)
    Aim:           Self
    Cooldown:      1000 ms
    Mana Cost:     10 MP
    Range:         4 units
    Damage:        25 (First strike) + 10 (Second strike)
    Radius:        4 units (First strike, 120 degree arc) / 4.5 units (Second strike, 180 degree arc)
    Effects:       Swift frontal strike dealing 25 damage. Automatically triggers a second
                   strike (Double Slash) 300 ms later, dealing 10 damage in a wider area.
                   Casting Smoke Teleport (R) during the 300 ms delay resets the timer and
                   prevents the second strike from firing.

  - [W] Shuriken Showdown
    Aim:           Direction
    Cooldown:      8000 ms
    Mana Cost:     40 MP
    Range:         30 units
    Damage:        35
    Projectile:    Speed 55, Range 30, Radius 0.8 (Piercing)
    Effects:       Throws a large shuriken that pierces all enemies in its path for 35 damage.

  - [E] Shadow Dash (Double Dash)
    Aim:           Direction
    Cooldown:      3000 ms (Single dash) / 6000 ms (Double dash)
    Mana Cost:     20 MP (First dash) + 10 MP (Second dash)
    Range:         6 units (First dash) + 6 units (Second dash)
    Effects:       Dashes forward 6 units (speed: 32). Initiates a recast window between
                   314 ms and 1700 ms after the cast.
                   Recasting E within this window performs a second dash (Double Dash),
                   grants a 25 HP shield for 3500 ms, and triggers a 6000 ms base cooldown.
                   If not recast, the ability triggers a 3000 ms base cooldown upon expiration.

  - [R] Smoke Teleport
    Aim:           Point
    Cooldown:      10000 ms
    Mana Cost:     60 MP
    Range:         10 units
    Damage:        35
    Radius:        3 units (AoE)
    Effects:       Teleports to a target area, dealing 35 damage and blinding enemies for 1500 ms.
